Master the Mosaic

Your guide to anchoring legacy across the grid.

Step 01

The Vision

Choose the image you want to immortalize. High-resolution works best. Whether it's a business logo, a graduation photo, or a GOAT, make sure it's worthy of the Vault.

Step 02

The Slice

To spread your image over multiple blocks, you need to split it. We recommend using Pinetoolsâ€”it's free, fast, and legendary.

  1. Go to Pinetools Split Image.
  2. Upload your photo.
  3. Select "Both (Grid)" in the options.
  4. Decide your size (e.g., 2x2 for 4 blocks, or 10x10 for 100 blocks).
  5. Click SPLIT IMAGE and download the ZIP file.
Step 03

The Anchor

Back in the Vault, select your starting block. Upload each "slice" to its corresponding coordinate. Once fixed, your image is part of the 1,000,000-block history.

Note: Each block is a separate $6 legacy. Plan your grid size according to your vision.
