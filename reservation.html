System Guide: Reservations & Login

The Vault of Time uses a specialized system to ensure fair access during reservations and secure access for owners.

Part 1: Buying & Reservations

  1. Select Your Blocks: Toggle "Multi-Select" (Shift+Click) to choose your blocks.
  2. Reserve: Click "Reserve All" and enter your details.
  3. The 2-Hour Window: The system locks these blocks for 2 hours. No one else can take them.
  4. Payment: You will receive a quote via email. Once paid, the blocks are permanently yours.
