The Reservation System

To ensure fairness and security for larger contributions to the Vault of Time, we utilize a specialized Reservation System for bulk purchases.

How It Works

  1. Select Your Blocks: Toggle "Multi-Select" and click the blocks you wish to own.
  2. Reserve: Click "Reserve All" and enter your details.
  3. The 2-Hour Window: The system instantly locks these blocks for 2 hours. During this time, no one else can purchase them.
  4. Quote & Payment: You will receive a generated quote and payment link in your email inbox.
  5. Confirmation: Once payment is verified, the reservation is lifted, and the blocks become permanently yours to build upon.

⚠️ Important

You have strictly 2 hours to complete the payment.
If payment is not received within this window, the system automatically releases the blocks back to the public pool.

Why is this necessary?

Because the Vault is a finite space of 1,000,000 blocks, managing large "neighborhoods" requires manual verification to prevent hoarding or accidental double-booking. This system allows you to secure your space immediately while we process the administrative details.
