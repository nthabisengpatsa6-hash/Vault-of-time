Vault Operations: Reservations & Ownership

Everything in the Vault is built for longevity. To ensure the integrity of the archive, we follow a strict "Proof of Payment" protocol.

Part 1: Reservation Protocols

Step 1: Single Block Claims

When you select an individual block, you are in "Preview Mode." The block is still open to the public until you act.

  • The 30-Minute Lock: To secure a block, you must click the "Reserve for 30 Minutes" icon within the block's selection window.
  • Manual Activation: If you do not click the icon and complete the payment immediately, the block remains live. Another user can claim it while you are still looking at it.

Step 2: Bulk & Range Selection (Multi-Select)

For those building larger legacies or mosaics, the Range Selection tool is mandatory:

  1. Toggle the "Enable Multi-Select" box in the menu.
  2. Click the first block of your desired area.
  3. Click "Mark Start" on the selection bar.
  4. The system will prompt you for the end of the range. Click the last block you want.
  5. Click "Reserve Range."
  6. Enter your Name and Email Address. A formal quote will be dispatched to your inbox immediately.

Receiving a quote does not reserve your blocks. In the interest of fairness and Firestore efficiency, bulk blocks remain open to the public until a transaction is time-stamped. If someone else claims and pays for those blocks before your funds clear, the blocks are theirs. Move fast.
