System Guide: Reservations & Login

The Vault of Time uses a specialized system to ensure fair access during reservations and secure access for owners.

Part 1: Buying & Reservations

Step 1: Selecting Blocks

You can select blocks in two ways:

  • Individual Selection: Simply click on any empty block to select it.
  • Range Selection (New): To select a large area at once:
    1. Toggle "Multi-Select" ON.
    2. Click your starting block.
    3. Click the "1. Mark Start" button on the floating bar.
    4. Click your ending block. All blocks in between will be selected automatically.

Step 2: Reservation Timers

Once you click "Reserve," your blocks are locked so no one else can take them while you pay. The duration of this lock depends on your order size:

Part 2: Owner Login & Editing

Once you own a block, you can edit its content at any time using our secure login system.
