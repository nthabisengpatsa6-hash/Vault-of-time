1. The Asset & Ownership

  • Nature of Goods: The Vault consists of 50 finite digital coordinates. Ownership of a coordinate grants The Keeper the exclusive right to display one (1) static image or one (1) audio file on that specific block for the duration of their tenure.
  • No Equity: Ownership of a coordinate does not constitute equity in Vault of Time (Pty) Ltd, nor does it grant rights to the underlying source code or infrastructure of the website.
  • Sovereignty: The Keeper retains full intellectual property rights to the content they upload, provided they are the legal creator or copyright holder.

2. The Ratchet Auction

  • Binding Bids: All applications to bid are binding contracts. By submitting a bid value, you commit to transferring the funds within 48 hours if selected.
  • Price Discovery: The base price of any available block is determined by the sale price of the previously sold block, plus a mandated 10% premium.
  • Non-Payment Penalty: Failure to complete the wire transfer/EFT within 48 hours of invoice issuance will result in the forfeiture of the bid and a permanent ban from The Vault.

3. Refund Policy & Cancellations

Due to the irreversible nature of digital access credentials and the immediate public exposure of the asset, ALL SALES ARE FINAL.

  • No Cooling-Off Period: By participating in this auction, you acknowledge that the transaction falls under the auction exclusion of the Consumer Protection Act (CPA). There is no "cooling-off" period once the bid is accepted.
  • Immediate Consumption: The service is deemed "rendered in full" the moment the Access Key is transmitted to your registered email address.
  • Buyer’s Remorse: Requests for refunds based on "change of mind," financial regret, or dissatisfaction with the public reception of your content will be summarily rejected.

4. Curation & The "Vibe Check"

The Curator reserves the absolute right to reject content based on aesthetic or ethical standards.

  • Prohibited Content: Hate speech, pornography, political propaganda, defamatory material, or low-resolution "junk" data is strictly prohibited.
  • The Kill Switch: The Curator reserves the right to unpublish any coordinate that violates these standards post-upload. No refunds will be issued if your content is removed due to a violation of Vault Standards.

5. Transfer & Secondary Market

  • The Ledger: Ownership is recorded internally in the Vault's Ledger. A Keeper may sell their coordinate to a third party.
  • The Royalties: Vault of Time (Pty) Ltd charges a 10% commission fee on the gross value of any secondary market sale or transfer.
  • Transfer Mechanism: Transfers are manual. The Curator must facilitate the handover of credentials to ensure the integrity of the chain of custody.
