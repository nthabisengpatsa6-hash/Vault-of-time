2. The Sealing & The Key

In the Vault, we don't do passwords—we use Magic Links. Your email is your biometric key. Once a block is purchased and "Sealed," it is permanently locked to your Unique Identity (UID).

Your contact details are tucked into a Private Envelope, invisible to the public, ensuring your privacy is as preserved as your memory.

3. Scarcity & Value

Only 1,000,000 blocks will ever exist. Once Chapter 1 is sealed, it is permanent. Think of it as digital biltong—carefully preserved, getting better with time, and definitely not for everyone.

4. The Final Goal

When Block 1,000,000 is placed, the Vault is archived forever as a permanent digital monument. You aren't just buying pixels; you're securing a coordinate in human history.
